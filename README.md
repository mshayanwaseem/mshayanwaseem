## About

I am a student and self-taught developer from Pakistan, building toward a career at the intersection of software engineering, AI tools, and product development.

Right now I am in the learning phase — writing code, building projects, studying design systems, and documenting what I figure out along the way. I am not an expert. I am someone who decided to build seriously and is doing the work.

My long-term direction is building software products and SaaS tools. I am approaching that goal the same way any good engineer does: by shipping things, learning from them, and repeating.
